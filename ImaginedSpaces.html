Imagined Spaces

Date: February 2022
Type: Identity, Research, Intervention
Collaborator: Joe Crossen
Submission: RSA Awards

Imagined Spaces is a platform that creates the capablity of local communities to discuss and re-imagine the future of derelict and disused spaces in their area.





Onsite interventions suggest possible uses for the derelict space and prompt the audience to suggest their own opinions.






Imagined Spaces supplies users with a free toolkit that can be easily customised to suit their own communities.







Imagined Spaces transcends current dereliction related platforms by creating:

01. Onsite interventions.
02. A microsite that publishes suggestions.
03. A toolkit to be used globally.
